About Prewave

Prewave is a leading brand in supply chain risk intelligence, offering comprehensive solutions for purchasing, supply chain management, and sustainability managers. With their innovative Prewave Action Platform, they provide a 360° view of supplier monitoring, ensuring transparency and minimizing risk. Using advanced artificial intelligence technology, Prewave scans millions of sources in over 50 languages to find, understand, and categorize risks that may affect your suppliers.


Their platform monitors social media and other relevant channels, sending you highly specific and relevant alerts. With a coverage of more than 250,000 direct and indirect suppliers, Prewave enables you to make informed decisions and take proactive measures before risk events occur. Their predictive analytics reports on potential risk events, allowing you to address them before they impact your operations.


Prewave's platform is multilingual, analyzing texts in local languages to gain a deeper understanding of potential risks. Their machine-readable alerts provide structured data points with all relevant attributes extracted, ensuring easy integration with your existing software systems. Stay ahead of the curve with Prewave's comprehensive supply chain risk intelligence solutions and protect your business from potential disruptions.
